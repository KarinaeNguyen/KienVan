# Kiến Văn Books — Brand Guideline (Summary)

This brand guideline for **Kiến Văn Books** outlines the visual identity, philosophy, and technical standards for the brand.

---

## I. Logo Meaning

The name "Kiến Văn" represents broad knowledge and deep understanding.

- **Kiến**: To see, to know, and to expand one’s horizons.
- **Văn**: To listen, to learn, and to absorb knowledge from others.
- **Combined Meaning**: Knowledge accumulated through the experiences of reading, listening, and learning.

The logo icon incorporates three key visual elements:

- **A Book**: Representing reading and literature.
- **A Lightbulb**: Representing knowledge and enlightenment.
- **Brand Name**: "Kiến Văn Books".

---

## II. Logo Standards

The logo is available in three primary color versions to ensure versatility across different applications:

- **Logo on White Background**: Standard full-color version.
- **Logo on Blue Background**: Inverted or light version for dark backgrounds.
- **Negative Space (Monochrome)**: For specific printing or high-contrast needs.

### Usage Restrictions

To maintain brand integrity, the following actions are strictly prohibited:

- **Distorting**: Stretching or squeezing the logo.
- **Adding Outlines**: Applying borders or strokes to the logo.
- **Changing Colors**: Using colors outside of the official palette.

---

## IV. Typography

The brand utilizes the **Be Vietnam Pro** font family for all communication materials:

- **Titles & Headings**: Be Vietnam Pro (Bold) with letter spacing of -40.
- **Body Text**: Be Vietnam Pro (Regular) with letter spacing of -40.

---

## V. Communication Materials

The guidelines provide examples for physical assets, such as **Business Cards**:

- **Front Side**: Features the brand logo prominently.
- **Back Side**: Contains personnel names (e.g., Duy Thành, Trần Chiến) and contact details.
- **Closing**: Includes a "Thank You" design for brand interaction.
